How to run it

  • Progression ruleWhen you hit the top of a rep range with good form & no back flare, add the smallest load next session. Reps before weight.
  • The back testIf a movement causes sharp or lingering low-back pain (not muscle fatigue), stop the set. Soreness next day is fine; pain that lingers 48h+ means regress.
  • BracingEvery hinge and carry: exhale, ribs down, brace like someone's about to poke your stomach. This is your insurance.
  • One hard day maxOnly the heaviest gym day should feel genuinely tough early on. Build, don't grind.
  • TrackingLog weight × reps (or time) each session. Everything saves to your Supabase backend and syncs across phone and laptop. The status line up top shows "Synced" when it's saved. Offline? It caches on the device and pushes once you're back.
